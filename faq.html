Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I set up my business in the UAE?
You can set up your business either as a mainland company or as a freezone company. Set Up.
How long does my company formation take?
Company formation may take from one week to a month. Our expertise can help cut it short to as low as three days. Contact us.
What are the procedures for business incorporation in the UAE?
The procedures for business incorporation in the UAE are subject to the requirements of each emirate. However, in general, businesses are incorporated in the UAE through one of two options:
  • Identify the nature of the activity.
  • Determine the legal structure.
  • Register the trade name.
  • Request for initial approval.
  • Obtain a security clearance.
  • Choose the location or address of the commercial activity.
  • Obtain additional government approvals.
  • Submit documents and pay the required fees.
Contact us.
What are the taxes and charges imposed on companies and on income in the UAE?
There are no taxes imposed on companies or on income in the UAE, but Corporate Tax Law will apply from 1 June 2023 with a 9% rate.
Does an individual need to be a UAE resident to qualify as a partner or a manager of a limited liability company (LLC) in the UAE?
The Commercial Companies Law does not require a partner or a manager of a limited liability company to be a resident of the UAE.
Can a foreign investor have full ownership in all sectors or specific sectors only?
Companies and investors of various nationalities may have full ownership of all economic activities in accordance with the activities identified by the competent local authorities and the list of strategic impact activities.
Does full foreign company ownership include all forms of legal structures?
Companies and investors of various nationalities may assume ownership of all legal structures mentioned in the Commercial Companies Law, namely public joint stock companies, private joint stock companies, limited liability companies, limited partnership companies, and partnership companies.
Is it required to appoint a UAE national agent for branches of foreign companies?
No, a UAE national agent is not required to be appointed by foreign companies wishing to conduct their activities within the UAE.
Is it a requirement for the Chairman and the majority of Board members to be Emirati nationals?
It is not a requirement for the Chairman and the majority of Board members to be Emirati nationals, with due consideration to the requirement set by the Council of Ministers or the competent authority, pursuant to Article (10) regarding strategic impact activities.
Is it permissible for a foreign investor to own a single-shareholder limited liability company?
Yes, a single natural or legal person may incorporate and own a limited liability company; and shall not be liable for its obligations, except to the extent of the capital stated in the articles of incorporation, while being subject to the provisions applicable to limited liability companies.
What are the requirements for establishing a branch of a foreign company?
The requirements include obtaining initial approval, completing the registration form, reserving a trade name, providing copies of IDs, and other necessary documentation.
What is the maximum number of licenses that may be acquired by a foreigner?
There is no maximum limit.
What are the requirements for issuing a Golden Visa to an investor in public investments?
The requirements include a deposit of at least two million dirhams, establishing or partnering in a company with a capital of not less than two million dirhams, and other similar provisions.
